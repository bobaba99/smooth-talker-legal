## 1. What Suavi does

Suavi is an iOS keyboard app that uses AI to rewrite your draft messages. When you tap the "Smooth" button, your draft text is sent to our server, processed by an AI model (Anthropic Claude), and three rewrite variants are returned to you. You choose which variant (if any) to send.
